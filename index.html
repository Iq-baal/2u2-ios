Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is 2U2.ai free to use? +

Yes, the basic engine is free for everyone. We believe memeing is a fundamental right. However, Power Users (removal of watermarks, high-res downloads, video generation) will need Credits, which can be earned via community contributions or acquired.

Can I use my own NFT? +

Absolutely. 2U2.ai is built for Web3. You can upload your own assets or connect your wallet (coming soon) to use your PFPs as the main character in our meme templates.

How do I partner with 2U2.ai? +

We are actively looking for ecosystem partners (L1/L2 chains, NFT collections, DeFi protocols). If you want a custom meme engine for your community, click the "Partner Inquiry" button above.
